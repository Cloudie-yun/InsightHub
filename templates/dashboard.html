Pro tip - asking questions

Be specific for better answers

Instead of "summarise this", try "What are the three main risks mentioned in section 2?" Specific questions get precise, grounded answers.

Pro tip - multi-file

Compare documents in one chat

Upload multiple files in a single conversation to ask cross-document questions and compare contracts, reports, or research papers side by side.

Pro tip - citations

Ask for sources every time

Add "cite the page and section" to any question so the system references exact passages you can verify directly in the document.

Pro tip - export

Copy answers as formatted text

Responses support markdown, so headers, bullets, and bold text carry into Notion, Word, or email with minimal cleanup.
